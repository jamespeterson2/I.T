Austin Nelson

This group assignment went really well overall. Each member of our group was present for all meetings, communication was kept the whole time and clear goals and times for work to be completed were set. There were never any unrealistic deadlines or amounts of work for any individual. Even if somebody got assigned a large chunk of work someone else would join in and lend a hand. Overall, I think the work was evenly distributed. I can’t complain at all with any of the effort put in by any members of our group. We did have some struggles in certain sections, however. Overall, our groups knowledge of GitHub, HTML and CSS languages were lacking, and this section of the assignment could be difficult at times. Other than that, the only issue we had was sometimes forgetting to record our meetings. I personally had never used MS Teams before this assignment and now feel a lot more confident in using it and all the tools that come with it. When I was setting up the interview with our IT professional, I realised how useful the recording and transcript features of Teams is especially when it comes to an assignment like this.
